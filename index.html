About Me

My Journey With Nature

Since childhood, I've been fascinated by the natural world. What began as simple hikes with a point-and-shoot camera has evolved into Click.it Ayushi - my premium photography brand dedicated to capturing Earth's most breathtaking landscapes and wildlife moments.

My work has taken me from the Himalayan peaks to the Western Ghats rainforests, always seeking to reveal nature's hidden beauty through my lens. I believe photography can inspire conservation and deepen our connection to the natural world.
